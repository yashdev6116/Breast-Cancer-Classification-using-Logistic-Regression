Breast Cancer Classification:


There are two types of Tumor:
1. Bengin Tumor
2. Malignant Tumor

1. Bengin Tumor - Non Cancerous
                  Slow growing

2. Malignant Tumor - Cancerous
                     Fast growing


Dataset:

Our dataset contains a column name as "Diagnosis"
where 0 represents - Malignant Tumor 
      1 represents - Bengin Tumor


Work-Flow:

1. Dataset/Data collection
2. Data pre processing
3. Spliting Data into Test, Train
4. Logistic Regression model (best for binarry classification)
5. Test model with new data
6. Evaluate
7. Result
